Study: 50 users with median age 25 over 5 weeks.
PANAS emotion states: you can feel both happy and sad at the same time similar to what we do in feelflow
## Findings
- [ ] We can predict user's emotional states by reading their notifications and their interaction with them with over 70% accuracy
- [ ] Not all notifications treated the same way by users. Notifications from people and events from real world are received positively
- [ ] Notifications sent by systems/non-humans are treated negatively. Basically people want to interact with a human being.
- [ ] The number of emojis correlates to positive feelings
- [ ] Frequency, Content, Importance and Source matter: Lots of notifications from WhatsApp group message will be low. If the content is just a marketing tool for apps to make users use it more. Email, WhatsApp, ... grab more attention than a promo code
- [ ] Notifications might cause ADHD-like symptoms
