My main goal is to see how LLM helped them with intervention and if we can add it to our smart notification proposal and change user's mental states and behaviors for better mental health similar to how they used llm for persuasive intervention.

Existing apps: 
1) Self-monitoring apps offer insights via notification and usage patterns (Promote self-awareness)
2) Apps with reminders: countering immediate phone indulgence (basically mom-nagging mode)
Easy to ignore
3) Interaction friction: give users challenges before they can use the app/phones
4) Lockout: disabling phones for specified period
Frustrating to use

Mindshift is based on the idea/research that persuasion through natural language works better to influence people's thoughts and behaviors. Current research and implementations use personalized context-aware interventions but they are limited to template-based repetitive reminders.

Mental factors like stress and negative emotions are key factor to problematic smartphone use.


**Mindshift**: Phone usage, physical context, mental states and long term user goals and habbits. +22.5% and around 5% across the board

Mindshift-Simple: Phone usage, physical context and long term user goals and habbits. (No mental state.) +17.8%


Study duration is 5 weeks and 25 participants.
